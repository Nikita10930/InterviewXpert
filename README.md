#  Interview Expert

#  Abstract
Interview Expert is a smart preparation system designed to assist candidates in improving their interview skills through structured question practice and guided learning. The system focuses on enhancing confidence, identifying weak areas, and providing a streamlined approach to interview readiness.

#  Application
- Helps students prepare for technical and HR interviews  
- Useful for placement training and self-assessment  
- Can be extended for company-specific interview preparation  
- Supports structured and consistent learning  

#  Introduction
In today’s competitive job market, interview preparation plays a critical role in securing opportunities. Many candidates lack proper guidance, structured practice, and feedback mechanisms.The Interview Expert system addresses this gap by providing an organized platform where users can practice interview questions, understand concepts, and improve their overall performance.

#  Literature Review
Existing platforms provide interview preparation resources such as question banks and mock interviews. However, many of them:
- Lack personalization  
- Do not provide structured learning paths  
- Offer limited feedback mechanisms  
This project aims to overcome these limitations by introducing a more organized and scalable solution.

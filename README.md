# 🎯 Interview Expert

## 📖 Problem Statement
Preparing for interviews can be overwhelming due to a lack of structured guidance, relevant questions, and real-time feedback. Many candidates struggle to identify their weak areas and improve effectively.

**Interview Expert** is designed to help users practice interview questions, improve their confidence, and get structured preparation support in a simple and efficient way.
